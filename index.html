Why human review matters

AI gathers the data.
Humans make it trustworthy.

Raw data from competitor monitoring is noisy. Ads get miscategorised. Website changes are trivial. Posts go viral for the wrong reasons. Automated tools miss the nuance — and you end up acting on bad intel.

At Marktel, every briefing passes through an experienced analyst before it reaches you. They filter out the noise, add strategic context, and flag what actually deserves your attention.
